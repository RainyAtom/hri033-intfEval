TITLE OF PROJECT: Robotics Interface Evaluation

PURPOSE OF THE RESEARCH:

This session is being conducted to evaluate the design of a robotics interface.


PROCEDURES TO BE USED:

If you decide to participate, you will be asked to provide demographic information. Then you will be presented with videos of two robotics interfaces and will be asked to answer a number of questions regarding each video. In order to progress through the study and receive your compensation code, you will be required to answer every question unless stated otherwise.The session will take approximately 10 minutes to complete.


RISKS/DISCOMFORTS INHERENT IN THE PROCEDURES:

There are no foreseeable risks beyond those of everyday life.


BENEFITS:

You will receive $2.00 for completion of the study. To prove you have completed the study, you'll receive a completion code at the end of the study to be used in the Prolific app.


CONFIDENTIALITY:

Your responses will be recorded but linked neither to personally identifiable nor worker information. The information will be kept strictly confidential and used only for research purposes. The documentation will be identifiable only by a randomly assigned ID number. All information will be kept on a secure web server. Sharing of data with other researchers will only be done in such a manner that you will not be identified. By participating, you understand and agree that the data and information gathered during this study may be used by Mines and published and/or disclosed by Mines to others outside of Mines. However, your name, contact information, and other direct personal identifiers will not be mentioned in any such publication or dissemination of the research data and/or results by Mines. Note that per regulation all research data must be kept for a minimum of 3 years, and are expected to be maintained indefinitely.


VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION AND WITHDRAWAL:

Your participation is completely voluntary. Should you decide at any time you no longer wish to participate, you may discontinue your participation without penalty. To withdraw from this experiment, please close the experiment window.


QUESTIONS

If you have questions or concerns about this research please contact the principal investigator: Tom Williams, MIRROR Lab, Colorado School of Mines, 1600 Illinois St. Golden, CO, twilliams@mines.edu
For questions, concerns, suggestions, or complaints that are not being addressed by the researcher, or research related harm, contact the Colorado School of Mines Human-Subject Research Office at humansubjects@mines.edu
